    What is Will Forte best known for?

    He resides in Santa Monica, California. He bought his home there just two weeks before joining the cast of Saturday Night Live, which necessitated a move to New York City.

    After graduating from UCLA with a degree in History and briefly working at a brokerage house, Forte decided to pursue comedy. He joined the world-famous improv group The Groundlings and was later hired as a writer for various series.
