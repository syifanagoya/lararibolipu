    Black Friday has officially commenced, and TBH, I can’t believe I just said that. Why? Because I’m in denial that this year is almost over — and Black Friday signals the start of that. So much happens post Turkey feast — my birthday (lol), Christmas, and the new year (!!). It’s like after Thanksgiving, the rest of the year is just a blur, and I simply can’t keep track. The upside to all of it? Well, really good deals. And a really damn good deal is happening at M.Gemi; in fact, it just dropped, and so did my jaw, with savings up to 60 percent off everything. 

    If you’re not familiar with M.Gemi, it’s time that you get to know it, especially if you like really good, really stylish shoes (like me!). The Italian footwear brand has been around for a while, and though initially best known for its famous, always-sold-out Greta clog, it offers much more than that. Stylish heels? Oh you bet M.Gemi has that, with pumps, heeled booties, and more designed with architectural heels that could easily be a work of art. In fact, some of my M.Gemis are on full display on a shelf because that’s how beautiful they are. 

    Best M.Gemi Black Friday Deals

    Beauty aside, all of M.Gemi’s footwear is also incredibly well designed (as you’d expect from Italians) and also super, duper comfy. Trust me, I’ve worn mine for everything, and the styles didn’t hurt my blister-prone feet right out of the box. I’ve tested a slew of shoes, from heels to boots to flats, and every single one has been *chef’s kiss.* 

    As a final cherry on top? The brand has earned the admiration of countless celebs. Gigi Hadid regularly steps out in the brand’s sandals; Zoe Saldaña wore the exact Danza flats that I was living in all summer long, and seeing her in the black pair naturally made me also want that colorway; and Lucy Hale and Camila Cabello have also opted for the brand’s summer sandal styles. 

    Whether you’re looking for a sensible flat (like these Danza flats that I got my sister obsessed with as well), a non-boring heel that’s certain to stop people in their tracks, or an edgy pump that’ll level-up all of your outfits, M.Gemi’s epic Black Friday sale has it all. The only thing you need to worry about? No buying too many pairs, though I (err, the shoe addict in me) fully supports you doing just that. Happy shoe shopping!
