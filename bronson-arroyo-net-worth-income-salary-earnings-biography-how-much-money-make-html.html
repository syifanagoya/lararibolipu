    When Arroyo was younger, his family relocated from Key West to Brooksville, where he attended Hernando High School. His professional baseball career started when he was drafted by the Pittsburgh Pirates in the Major League Baseball 1995 Draft. He made his MLB debut with the club in 2000. In his first season, Bronson had a record of 2-6 in the 20 games he played, and in his second season, he had a record of 5-5 with an earned run average of 5.0In his third season, he had a record of 5-5 with an earned run average of 5.0In 2003, he was dealt to the Boston Red Sox, where he remained for three seasons. In his last season with the Red Sox, in 2005, Arroyo made 35 appearances, went 14-10, and allowed just five stolen bases in his final season. ‘’Covering the Bases” was the title of his cover album, which was published in the same year. The Red Sox signed him to a three-year contract for $11.25 million prior to the start of the 2006 season; however, he was moved to the Cincinnati Reds in 2006, and the team finished the season with a record of 14-10, earning him the Most Valuable Player award for his contributions in the process. In 2007, he received a contract extension with the club, and he finished the season with a 4.23 earned run average and a 9-15 win-to-loss record. 

    The next year, he threw 200 innings and had a 4.77 earned run average — he gave up 11 earned runs in a game against the Toronto Blue Jays. After being diagnosed with carpal tunnel syndrome the next year, Bronson missed many games as a result of his health issue. However, he returned to the club for the second half of the 2009 season and completed the season with an ERA of 3.84 and a record of 15-1In 2010, he was selected to be the number two starter for his team’s postseason series against the New York Mets. In 2011, he agreed to a contract extension for $35 million, which would have allowed him to stay with the club for an additional three years after that. In the same season, he was diagnosed with mononucleosis and was unable to return to the field until he was completely healed, resulting in a 5.07 earned run average for the season.
