    Engaged to Be Murdered cast are Sarah-Jane Redmond, Erin Boyes, Madison Smith, and more. The Lifetime movie is set to release on New Years Day in 2023.

    The movie is directed by well-known actress Keegan Connor Tracy; she has directed films such as Scented With Love and Cradle Did Fall, and the production company that worked on the movie was Champlain Media. According to IMDB, the title of the film is also known as “Meet My Mother.”

    According to Keegan’s Instagram post, the shooting of the movie was completed in 12 days. Engaged to Be Murdered is about a mother who would do anything to stop her son from getting married to his girlfriend.

    The film will premiere on January 1, 2023, on Lifetime Network, and you can catch up with it the next day as it will be streamed on the Lifetime App.

    Engaged to Be Murdered Lifetime Cast

    Lifetime film Engaged to Be Murdered has well-known faces, including Erin Boyes, Sarah-Jane Redmond, Madison Smith, and Malaika Jackson.

    Erin Boyes as Olivia Clark

    Boyes will be seen playing the role of Olivia Clark, who is the girlfriend of the main lead, Jack. Her character in the film is exciting and captivating, and as per the synopsis, she is not a favorite of her boyfriend’s mother.

    Erin recently appeared in the role of Savannah in the movie The Week They Won and as Dr. Laura Smiley in Dying for Chocolate: A Curious Caterer Movie. Throughout her acting career, she has worked with networks such as Lifetime, Hallmark, SyFy, and Netflix.

    As a producer, she has produced four short films, including Jane, Three Days, Fruitcake, and Nomansland. She received two LEO Award nominations for her performances in Waking Up to Danger and Three Days.

    Joy for Christmas, The Picture of Christmas, Love at the Cedar Creek, and Cranberry Christmas are among her other film credits.

    Sarah-Jane Redmond as Amanda Finley

    Redmond will be portraying the character of Amanda Finley, a mother who can go to any length to stop her son from marrying his lover.

    Previously, Sarah appeared as Ava Bell in the television movie A Royal Runaway Romance. She is also known for her recurring roles in shows such as Chilling Adventures of Sabrina, Siren, The 100, and Tribal.

    She has been in the industry for over 25 years and has appeared in more than 100 films. Her notable film and television credits include Millennium, The X-Files, Smallville, Alienated, Da Vinci’s City Hall, and Cedar Cove.

    Madison Smith as Jack Finley

    Smith will play the role of Jack Finley, and in the film, the two women in his life—his mother and girlfriend—don’t get along.

    Recently, he appeared as the main lead in the Christmas film Meeting Mr. Christmas. He also appeared in the crime-drama movie Nikki & Nora: Sister Sleuths as Robby Watts.

    He is also known for appearing in television shows such as The Order, Riverdale, Supergirl, and The Chilling Adventures of Sabrina. Smith has also acted in numerous films, including Psycho Intern, Falling for You, Frozen in Love, and Deadly Attraction.

    Madison will be playing the lead role in the film The Perfect Man(icure), which will be released in 2023.

    Malaika Jackson as Detective Armstrong

    Jackson will play a detective in the upcoming film Engaged to Be Murdered.

    Previously, in the films Let’s Get Physical and Color My World with Love, she had also portrayed the role of an officer. She is known for her acting in television shows such as The Baby-Sitters Club, Arrow, iZombie, and Untold Stories of the ER.

    She is also a pageant titleholder, having been crowned Miss Caribbean Canada. Malaika has appeared in over 50 commercials since the beginning of her acting career.

    Engaged to Be Murdered Storyline and Trailer

    Engaged to Be Murdered has a classic storyline where the guy’s mother is not fond of his girlfriend or partner. But in this film, the mom will go to any length to get her way.

    As per the synopsis provided by the Lifetime Network, the plot of this thriller movie is about a mother, Amanda Finley, who will try to stop her son, Jack Finley, from getting married to his lover, Olivia.

    Amanda believes that Olivia is marrying her son for money, and she feels protective as a mother. It looks like Amanda will make major life-changing decisions to protect her son from his lover.
